This application draws inspiration from popular gambling services (William Hill or Ladbrokes), only instead of money we play for points, although there may be a cash prize at the end of the game.

How to play?

Build your bet slip by adding your predictions for selected matches to it. You can add as many predictions to the slip as you wish.

Visit your slip in order to submit your predictions.

Points are awarded based on the following scoring system:

  • 3 points for a correct result (i.e. home/away/draw)

  • 1 point for correct home team goals

  • 1 point for correct away team goals

  • If you get the score line spot on, you’ll get all of the above (3 + 1 + 1 = 5 points)

Everyone gets three “Boosters” which they can elect to use on three fixtures of their choice. This wlil double the points scored for that fixture.

The player who collects the most points at the end of the game wins.

Good luck!
